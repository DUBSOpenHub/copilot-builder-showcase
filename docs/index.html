The awkward last ten minutes

Workshops are great at building. Bad at ending.

Links scatter across chat threads, docs, and browser tabs. A few people get demo time, most projects get no review, and the session just… stops. A human judging panel would fix it — but recruiting judges is too much overhead for most sessions.

🔗

Links everywhere

Project URLs sit in a spreadsheet or a chat thread and never get seen together.

🎤

Only a few demo

Time runs out. Most builders never get their moment on screen.

⚖️

Inconsistent review

Quality depends on who speaks up loudest, not one shared rubric.

🫥

No shared ending

Feedback and decisions disappear the moment everyone closes their laptop.

Run of show

Three moves from link list to trophy.

The whole thing runs on one screen the room watches together. Here is the flow you will drive on stage.

Scan to submit

A real, scannable QR code appears. Builders scan it, paste a project link, and their project lands on the board — project first.

  • ✓ Live QR generated on-device
  • ✓ Lightweight mobile submit view
  • ✓ Every accepted project gets a spotlight

Run the panel & reveal

A Copilot judging panel reviews every project through the same rubric, the room joins the cue, and the podium lands.

  • ✓ Builder Bronze · Silver · Copilot Builder Award
Fair by default

Credible judging, not a gimmick.

Copilot judging combines a configured Copilot CLI panel by median consensus. The audience view never leaks a result early.

🔒

Sealed scores

No scores, ranks, or awards on the big screen until the reveal.

✨

Equal spotlight

Same rubric

🤝

Median consensus

Evidence, not inference
